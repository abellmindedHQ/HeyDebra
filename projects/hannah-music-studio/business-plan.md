# Hannah Aldridge Music Studio — Business Plan

**What it is:** Private music lessons in guitar, piano, voice, and performance. Taught by a touring musician.

## The Point

Most music teachers either baby you or intimidate you. This isn't that.

This is lessons for people who want to feel capable and confident — not stressed or judged. Whether you're learning for fun, getting serious about performing, or finding your way back to music after years away, the approach is the same: honest, clear, grounded.

No hype. No false promises about "the industry." Just good teaching from someone who's been on stage and knows what actually matters.

## What Makes This Different

- Taught by a working, touring musician (not just someone who studied pedagogy)
- Strong focus on performance confidence, not just technique drills
- Safe, ethical approach — especially for vocal work (screening required to protect your voice)
- Real-world skills, not just recital factory pressure
- No one-size-fits-all curriculum

## What I Teach

**Instruments:** Beginner guitar, Beginner piano, Voice (all levels, screened), Performance coaching (all levels)

**Lesson lengths:** 30 min, 45 min, 60 min

**Schedule:** Monday–Thursday afternoons/early evenings. Fridays reserved for admin, rest, or my own work.

**Location:** Rush's Music — keeps overhead low, keeps pricing competitive.

## Who This Is For

- **Adult beginners** who always wanted to learn but feel intimidated or "too old"
- **Teen and adult vocalists** who've had bad instruction or need to rebuild confidence
- **Performing artists** who are stuck — good technique, shaky stage presence

## Pricing

**Single lessons:** 30 min: $40 | 45 min: $60 | 60 min: $85

**Monthly packages (preferred):**
- Weekly 30 min: $150/month
- Weekly 45 min: $240/month

Monthly subscriptions keep income stable and students consistent.
