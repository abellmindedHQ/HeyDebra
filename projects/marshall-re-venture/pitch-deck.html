The Strategy

Two asset classes.
One philosophy.

Grocery-Anchored Retail

  • Essential services = recession-proof (positive NOI during 2008 and COVID)
  • Long-term leases (10-20 years), NNN structures
  • Cap rates 6.4-6.8%, CBRE's #1 retail pick for the next decade
  • Limited new supply protects existing centers

Workforce Multifamily

  • Structural undersupply = pricing power
  • Price per unit ~$135K (value territory)
  • Strong rent growth with population influx
  • Multiple exit strategies (hold, refi, sell)
