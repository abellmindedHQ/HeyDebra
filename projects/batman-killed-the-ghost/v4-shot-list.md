# Batman Killed the Ghost v4 - THE COMEDY CUT
## Shot List & Comedic Notes

**THE FORMULA:** Epic 3-year-old narration + hilariously bad stick figure art + perfect comedic timing = funny

**STYLE RULES:**
- Literally looks like a 3-year-old drew it
- Stick figures. Circle heads. Stick arms from heads (no bodies sometimes)
- Batman = circle + two triangles on top + cape scribble
- Spider-Man = stick figure colored red and blue
- Ghost = white blob with two dot eyes
- Houses = square + triangle roof
- Everything on white paper with crayon
- Colors go outside the lines AGGRESSIVELY
- Some things are labeled in terrible kid handwriting because the drawing isn't clear enough
- Scale is wrong. Batman is bigger than the house sometimes.

**FRAME 12 (0:52-0:58)** — "the ghost was alive again"

**FRAME 13 (0:58-1:02)** — "all of them were"

**FRAME 14 (1:02-1:10)** — "a glowing book came in the night"

**FRAME 16 (1:15-1:22)** — "Batman ran away over the fences back to his house"

**FRAME 17 (1:22-1:27)** — "and never came back again"
Drawing: Batman inside his house. door shut. a lock on the door (big circle with a keyhole). he's peeking through a tiny window with terrified eyes. outside: the ghost is just standing there. waving.

**FRAME 18 (1:27-1:30)** — "and that's the end"

**FRAME 19 (1:30-1:33)** — Alex: "That's the end?"

**FRAME 20 (1:33-1:40)** — "and they happily lived ever again"

### ACT 3: MAGIC SPARKLES

**FRAME 27 (2:04-2:10)** — "Magic sparkles trying to get other sparkles"

**FRAME 28 (2:10-2:14)** — Alex: "I want to hear that one" / Avie: "Okay"
Drawing: dad stick figure and Avie stick figure. dad looks intrigued. Avie clears her throat (action lines around her mouth).

**FRAME 29 (2:14-2:20)** — "One time it was a night day"
Drawing: the page is literally split in half diagonally. one half colored black (night), one half colored yellow (day). this is "a night day." genius 3-year-old worldbuilding. a sun AND a moon at the same time.

**FRAME 30 (2:20-2:25)** — "it was glowed outside"
Drawing: same split sky but now everything has yellow scribble lines radiating off it. EVERYTHING glows. the trees glow. the ground glows. it just "glowed outside."

**FRAME 31 (2:25-2:32)** — "sparkles came hopping along inside the forest"
Drawing: little round circle creatures with dot eyes and stick legs HOPPING through scribble trees. they have no arms. they're just circles with legs. bouncy arc lines show them hopping.

**FRAME 32 (2:32-2:42)** — "they hopped hopped hopped hopped trying to get the other ones"
Drawing: sparkle chase scene! front sparkles are running (speed lines), back sparkles are chasing (angry eyebrows). every "hopped" could be a tiny sub-frame where they move slightly. the forest is just green scribbles.

**FRAME 33 (2:42-2:47)** — "because the other ones were running away"
Drawing: the running-away sparkles look back with terrified dot eyes. one is tripping. another is hiding behind a tree (poorly, you can see it sticking out).

**FRAME 34 (2:47-2:53)** — "the ghost tried to scare the sparkles"
Drawing: THE GHOST FROM STORY 1 MAKES A CAMEO. it pops up behind a tree going "BOO" (word bubble). the sparkles scatter in every direction. this is the CINEMATIC UNIVERSE.

**FRAME 35 (2:53-2:58)** — "the sparkles ran away back to their house"

**FRAME 36 (2:58-3:00)** — "Rainy day."

**FRAME 37 (3:00-3:07)** — "they fall asleep and then their moms and dads were so happy"
Drawing: inside the sparkle house. little sparkle circles in beds. bigger sparkle circles (with a bow and a hat to mark mom and dad) standing nearby with huge smiles. hearts everywhere.
