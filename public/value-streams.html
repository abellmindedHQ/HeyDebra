🏛️ Application Development

Value Stream Maps & Organizational Structure

Overview

The Application Development Division is structured around value-stream portfolios that align with ORNL's mission and operational needs. This design ensures clarity of purpose, efficient resource allocation, and strategic innovation.

🔺 Organizational Pyramid (Top to Bottom):
  • Division Director: Jay (Strategic Direction)
  • Performance, Innovation & Transformation: Alex Abell (Modernization, Performance, Innovation)
  • Three Value-Stream Portfolios: Run the Business, Accelerate the Mission, Engage the Community
  • Foundation (Shared Services): Brooks Herring (Experience & Quality)

Value-Stream Portfolios

1. Run the Business

Laboratory Operations & Institutional Support

Ensures the foundational systems that power ORNL's day-to-day operations and institutional efficiency.

Group Leader: Kim Smithson

Funding: Institutional / Overhead

Programs: Financial Management, HR & Talent Management, Procurement & Legal, Facilities & Asset Management, Safety, Security & Access

2. Accelerate the Mission 🔬

Research & Mission Systems

Delivers the digital capabilities that enable, manage, and accelerate ORNL's scientific research lifecycle and compliance.

Group Leader: Kevin Sellars

Funding: Programmatic mission funding + Lab investment

Programs: Research Lifecycle Management, Lab & Experiment Management, Research Compliance, Research Data & Tools

3. Engage the Community

Digital Experience & Collaboration

Connects the lab to its stakeholders through collaboration platforms and digital experiences that enable knowledge sharing and engagement.

Group Leader: [New GL Placeholder]

Funding: Mixed institutional funding + stakeholder cost-share

Programs: Knowledge & Content Management, Collaboration & Communications, Stakeholder Services

Shared Services: Experience & Quality

A center of excellence that ensures every application at ORNL is usable, accessible, and reliable.

Group Leader: Brooks Herring

Funding: Pooled across all portfolios

Functions

  • Experience Enablement: Design systems, usability testing, accessibility standards
  • Quality Assurance: Automation frameworks, testing practices, reliability standards

A shared enabling function, not a product team — amplifying the impact of limited UX and QA resources across all three portfolios.

Strategic Accelerator: Performance, Innovation & Transformation

Drives forward-looking capabilities, modernization, and continuous improvement across all portfolios.

Group Leader: Alex Abell

Core Functions

  • Transformation & Modernization (Center): Cloud adoption, DevOps, automation, agile practices
  • Performance (Left): Data Architecture, Application Architecture, Goal Measurement & Reporting
  • Innovation (Right): AI for Operations, emerging tech evaluation, pilot initiatives

Positioned horizontally to fuel innovation and optimization across the entire division while maintaining direct accountability to Jay.

How It All Fits Together

Portfolios deliver core value to the lab's operations, mission, and community.

Experience & Quality Group ensures outcomes are user-centered and reliable.

Performance, Innovation & Transformation Group ensures we are future-ready and continuously improving.

Together, this structure makes Application Development more than a delivery organization — it becomes a force multiplier for ORNL's mission.
