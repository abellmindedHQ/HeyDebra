### Tier 1: Superpower Session
**$500 — One-Time**

**What It Is:**
A 90-minute deep-dive session where Alex analyzes your business, project, or career — finds the thing that makes you genuinely different, and hands you a clear picture of how technology can scale it.

**Format:**
- 90 minutes, video call
- Alex does his analysis in real-time, out loud — you'll hear how he thinks
- Asynchronous prep: you fill out a short intake form before the call
- You leave with a written Clarity Document delivered within 48 hours

**Deliverables:**
1. **Clarity Document** — A written articulation of your superpower (the version you've been struggling to say out loud)
2. **3 Technology Amplification Opportunities** — Specific, prioritized recommendations for how technology can scale your reach, revenue, or impact
3. **Priority Stack** — Which of the 3 to pursue first, and why

**Ideal Client:**
- Solopreneurs and practitioners with deep expertise but unclear positioning
- Founders who know they're building something valuable but can't explain it to investors or customers
- Leaders stepping out of an institutional career (like a government job, academia, or corporate) into their own thing
- Anyone who has been told "you should charge more" but doesn't know how to justify it

**Why $500:**
This session was previously offered for free. It's $500 because Alex's time has value, because paid clients show up differently, and because $500 is a reasonable filter: if you can't invest $500 in clarity, you're probably not ready for what comes next.

**Upgrade Path:**
After a Superpower Session, clients who want ongoing strategy support are invited to the Amplify Retainer. The $500 is credited toward the first month.

## 3. The Freemium Funnel

### Concept: The AI Superpower Analysis

**The Problem This Solves:**
Most people won't pay $500 cold. They need to experience something before they buy. The freemium funnel gives them a taste of Alex's thinking — delivered instantly, at scale, by AI — with a clear path to going deeper.

**How It Works:**

**Step 1: The Form**
Visitor lands on `/consulting` and sees: *"Find out what your superpower actually is — free analysis in 24 hours."*
They fill out a short intake form (5–7 questions):
- What do you do / what are you building?
- Who do you help?
- What result do you consistently deliver?
- What do people thank you for most?
- What problem keeps you up at night?
- What have you tried that hasn't worked?
- What would "winning" look like in 12 months?

**Step 2: The AI Analysis**
Debra (Alex's AI assistant) processes the form and generates a 1–2 page Superpower Analysis:
- What the actual superpower appears to be (reframed in clear language)
- What's obscuring it (the positioning problem)
- One technology amplification idea to illustrate what's possible
- A note that this is a preliminary read — the full picture takes 90 minutes with Alex

This goes out within 24 hours. It's real analysis. Not a generic PDF.

**Step 3: The CTA**
Bottom of the analysis: *"This is what I could see from a form. Book a Superpower Session and we'll go three levels deeper — you'll leave with a full clarity doc and a prioritized technology roadmap. $500, 90 minutes."*

**Link:** Book directly via Calendly or similar.

**Why This Works:**
1. It's genuinely valuable — people will share it
2. It demonstrates Alex's thinking before they pay
3. It filters for real clients (people who fill out a thoughtful form are serious)
4. It's scalable — Debra handles the analysis, Alex handles the follow-through
5. It creates a natural conversation opener for warm outreach

**Funnel Metrics to Track:**
- Form completion rate
- Analysis delivery time
- Analysis → Session booking conversion rate (target: 10–15%)
- Session → Retainer conversion rate (target: 20–30%)

**Build Requirements:**
- Simple intake form (Typeform, Tally, or custom)
- AI analysis pipeline (OpenAI / Claude API + prompt template)
- Email delivery system (simple automated send)
- Calendly integration for booking

**Timeline to Build:** 1–2 weeks for MVP.

## 4. Case Studies

### Case Study 1: Executive Wellness Practice (Real: Heart Connected Healing)
*Note: Client is aware this work may be referenced. Suggest confirming explicit permission before publishing with full name and details. For now, framed as anonymized.*

**The Client:**
A licensed clinical psychologist with 20 years of specialized practice in an institutional setting. National rankings. Pioneer of integrated care programs that are now standard in her field. Ready to launch an independent practice — but couldn't articulate what made her different from any other therapist.

**The Problem:**
When asked to describe her practice, she defaulted to credentials and clinical language. The result: she sounded like everyone else. Her actual superpower — a deeply integrated, whole-person methodology developed over two decades at the institutional frontier — was invisible in her positioning.

**The Work:**
Alex worked with her to identify the core of her differentiation: it wasn't that she was a good psychologist. It was that she had pioneered a model of care that the institutional system had finally caught up with, and was now offering that model privately to clients who needed the real thing.

**The Outcome:**
- Launched her private practice with clear brand identity and differentiated positioning
- Shifted from "VA psychologist" to a premium practice with a defined methodology and client type
- Has a clear story to tell that matches the actual quality of her work

**The Lesson:**
The deepest expertise is often the hardest to see from the inside. The people who are most exceptional at what they do are frequently the worst at explaining why.
