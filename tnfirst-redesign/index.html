Three Pathways to Innovation

The FIRST progression of programs builds on itself, guiding students from playful discovery to real-world engineering.

🧱

FIRST LEGO League

Grades PreK-8

Introduces STEM to children ages 4-16 through hands-on learning. Three divisions inspire youth to experiment and grow their critical thinking, coding, and design skills through guided robotics challenges.

Learn More →
⚙️

FIRST Tech Challenge

Grades 7-12

Students think like engineers. Teams design, build, and program robots to compete in alliance format. Built on a reusable platform with Android technology and Java-based programming.

Learn More →
🤖

FIRST Robotics Competition

Grades 9-12

The ultimate challenge. Teams design, program, and build robots for head-to-head competition. Students also build a brand, develop community partnerships, and promote STEM locally.

About TNFIRST

Our Mission

TNFIRST, Inc. is a tax-exempt nonprofit focused on connecting K-12 students to the world of FIRST robotics. We believe students achieve their best when exposed to business, manufacturing, and technology during their formative years.

Using competitive robotics programs, we give ALL students the opportunity to solve problems, work in teams, and engage with every aspect of business and technology.
