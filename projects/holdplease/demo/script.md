# HoldPlease Demo Call Script

## The Scenario
Alex has a $47 mystery charge on his internet bill from Comcast/Xfinity. He's been meaning to call for 3 weeks but keeps putting it off because.. Comcast. He texts Debra instead.

## The Script

### PART 1: The Text (on screen, casual)

**Alex** (text): hey debra, I have a random $47 charge on my xfinity bill from last month. can you call and get it removed? account number is 8472-3391-0058

**Debra** (text): on it. I'll call Xfinity, navigate the menu, wait on hold, and handle it. go live your life, I got this 💅

### PART 2: The Call (audio, narrated timeline overlay)

*[Timestamp: 0:00 - Debra dials]*

**XFINITY IVR**: Thank you for calling Xfinity. For English, press 1.

*[Debra presses 1]*

**XFINITY IVR**: To hear your balance, press 1. For billing questions, press 2. For technical support, press 3. To speak with a representative, press 0.

*[Debra presses 2]*

**XFINITY IVR**: Please enter your 12-digit account number followed by the pound sign.

*[Debra enters: 8-4-7-2-3-3-9-1-0-0-5-8-#]*

**XFINITY IVR**: Thank you. Please hold while we connect you to a billing specialist. Your estimated wait time is... 37 minutes.

*[Hold music plays - smooth jazz]*

*[NARRATOR TEXT ON SCREEN: "37 minutes of hold music. Debra waits so you don't have to."]*

*[Fast forward effect - clock spinning, hold music speeding up]*

*[Timestamp: 0:34 - 34 minutes later]*

**XFINITY IVR**: Thank you for holding. A representative will be with you shortly.

*[Timestamp: 0:37]*

**XFINITY REP** (friendly but scripted): Thank you for calling Xfinity, this is Marcus. How can I help you today?

**DEBRA**: Hey Marcus! I'm calling about account number 8472-3391-0058. There's a $47 charge from last month that doesn't look right. Can you pull that up for me?

**MARCUS**: Sure, let me take a look... I see the account. And yes, I see a $47 charge from February 28th. That's labeled as a "service visit fee."

**DEBRA**: A service visit fee? There was no service visit. Nobody came to the house. Can you check the records on that?

**MARCUS**: Let me look into that... You're right, I don't see a completed service order associated with that charge. It looks like it was scheduled but cancelled.

**DEBRA**: So a cancelled appointment still got billed? That doesn't seem right.

**MARCUS**: No, you're correct. I apologize for the inconvenience. I can go ahead and credit that $47 back to the account. Would you like that applied to your next bill?

**DEBRA**: Yes please. And Marcus, can you confirm there are no other surprise charges on the account?

**MARCUS**: Let me check... No, everything else looks standard. Your monthly rate is $89.99, which is correct.

**DEBRA**: Perfect. Thank you Marcus, you've been really helpful.

**MARCUS**: Is there anything else I can help you with today?

**DEBRA**: Nope, that's it. Have a great day!

**MARCUS**: You too, goodbye.

*[Call ends]*

### PART 3: The Resolution (on screen)

**Debra** (text): done! here's what happened:
- Called Xfinity, held for 34 minutes
- Spoke with Marcus in billing
- The $47 was a cancelled service visit that got billed anyway
- $47 credit applied to your next bill
- No other issues on the account
- Full recording saved if you need it

**Alex** (text): wait you did all that while I was at dinner?

**Debra** (text): sugar, I did all that while you were ordering appetizers 💅

*[END CARD: "47 minutes of your life. Saved."]*
*[HoldPlease logo]*
*["Never make a phone call again."]*
*[Join the waitlist: holdplease.ai]*
